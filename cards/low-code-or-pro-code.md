# Low-Code or Pro-Code, in One Breath

**The deciding question:** does the job change the system of record under rules only you know? If yes, it is a code job.

## Lean low-code (Joule Studio) when

- You could describe the whole job in one sentence.
- It leans only on standard SAP data and actions.
- It mostly reads and advises; it does not change the system of record.
- The builders are business experts, and speed matters most.

## Reach for pro-code when

- The job needs careful, multi-step logic you control.
- It touches money or another system of record.
- It reaches into things that are not standard SAP.
- You must test it hard and prove it behaves, with a control layer you can read line by line.

Both roads are governed. The choice is fit, not safety. Most teams use both, and pick the road by the job in front of them.
